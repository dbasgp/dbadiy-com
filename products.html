What you can control, and from where

Included with every unit

  • Drain pump
  • UV-C sterilization lamp
  • Stainless steel mesh filter
  • Cold-rolled steel casing
  • Emergency stop button
  • Humidity sensor with 5 m cable
  • 86 × 86 mm LCD control panel with 8 m cable
  • Type G plug with 5 m cable
  • Remote controller

Control features

  • Set humidity 20 – 95 %
  • Fan speed: low / high
  • Fan and dehumidifying modes
  • Scheduled on/off timer
  • Fault detection & codes
  • Power-off memory
  • WiFi app control
  • Smart home / BMS via RS485 Modbus
  • Dry contact control input
  • Water leak sensor port
  • Fault indicator output (220 V, 6 A)

All three units share the same control logic and wiring scheme. Pick the unit on capacity and area, not on features.
